Your information matters

Privacy policy

Effective 17 July 2026

What RiseTogether stores

RiseTogether stores the account, profile, optional interests, posts, messages, Family activity, goals, reactions, referrals, point records, reports, notification preferences and security records needed to provide the features you choose to use. Uploaded photos, audio, video and documents are stored when you share them.

How information is used

Your information is used to operate conversations, Families, challenges, support features, safety moderation, notifications and account security. RiseTogether does not invent activity or memories and does not sell personal information.

Private content and location

Private messages are shown only to their participants and authorized safety processes. A location is shared only when you deliberately send it in a conversation. Notification previews can be disabled in Settings so private message text is not placed on the lock screen.

Push notifications

If you enable device notifications, RiseTogether stores a device subscription endpoint and encryption keys. You can disable that device in Settings or remove browser permission. Expired subscriptions are deactivated automatically.

Interests, referrals and points

Optional interests are used only to show matching visible posts in the “My interests” feed. Referral records connect an invitation to a new account and count distinct active days before a reward is issued. Point transactions are calculated and logged by the server to prevent duplicate or fraudulent rewards.

Your choices

You can edit your profile visibility, post audience, notification categories, check-in preferences and message-preview preference. You can block users, report unsafe content, download media you received, or permanently delete your account from Settings.

Safety and retention

Records are retained while needed to provide the service, protect users, resolve reports and meet legal obligations. Account deletion removes data covered by database delete rules; limited security or audit records may be retained when necessary to prevent abuse.

Questions

Use the RiseTogether help system for a privacy question or deletion problem.
